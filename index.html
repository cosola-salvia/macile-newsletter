We are the Adventurers

The MACILE Summer Academy is a rigorous five week program in grades 6 to 12. The curriculum includes math, science, engineering, technology and language. Courses and activities are designed to stimulate curiosity, creativity and passion for learning; also to build the students' skills, analytical abilities and rational thinking. The program was piloted in july 2007 and 2008.
