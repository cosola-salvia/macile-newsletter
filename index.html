Conquered Steps

The lessons we learned

This is the story of Us; a group of young people from less privileged communities, who are blessed with immense talents and virtues, and who, motivated by the passion to discover and learn, undertook the path of education, determined to achieve their goals. Each step conquered is a testament to the perseverance and determination that characterizes each one of us.

Each one of us have a different story, but all of us have had to learn the art of discipline and recognize that talent alone is not enough for success. Talent without discipline is like a ship on the ocean without a compass or GPS. Climbing each step demanded hard and constant work, passion and resilience. Each failure opened opportunities. Each achievement was a call to persevere.

In addition to attending our respective schools, every Saturday, every summer, and every Christmas break we attended the MACILE Academy, taking preparatory lessons to advance our learning. Some of us also studied English, making great strides and achieving competence in a short time.

"Congratulations for the courage and job well-done" - familia MACILE

"The MACILE family congratulates each of you scholars for your courage and the job well done. Congratulations!"

We are natives of San Gregorio de Nigua, Bajos de Haina and Madre Vieja in the Province of San Cristóbal, Dominican Republic.

Our Common Story

This is our common story, that at different points in time each of us was admitted to the MACILE Summer Academy. We are also MACILE scholars. MACILE is an education program of Complex Systems Optimization Lab (COSOLA). It stands for Mathematics, Sciences, Engineering, and Language, in Spanish. MACILE is dedicated to advance engineering education in less advantaged communities. It has operated in our region of Itabo-San Gregorio de Nigua since 2007. MACILE nurtures the talents of young people from poor backgrounds, like us, who love math and seek to pursue studies and careers in STEM related fields.
